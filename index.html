FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I receive real honey for stacking THJ Tokens?

Yes. We unite the digital with the real world by providing real pure honey made by the bees, for every hive our adopters are stacking THJ Tokens.
‍
Example: for stacking 50,000 THJ Tokens (one hive) your reward is an NFT with jar of real honey. Every month.

Is there an annual maintenance fee?

Yes but optional. All the adopters can continue after the 2nd year of adoption with a small fee of only € 55 per year,

ensuring to keep the real beekeepers in place with their real hives, take care of the bees and produce real honey. This way adopters keeping their adopter privilege and continue to receive top quality honey NFTs

How much it costs to withdraw honey?

Every user pays the shipping and packaging costs. Receiving the honey to your physical address depends on the packaging type and the shipping company of
‍
your choosing. We fulfill every order and give the goods to any courier/shipping company you send to pick it up. We agree you can to use any shipping company you prefer (DHL, FEDEX, ANY). So we recommend to contact and ask the company you choose for this service. *info: we ship from our hubs and deposits in Netherlands, Germany, Serbia and Romania

Is the honey tested?

Yes, all our honey is tested in EU using more than one laboratory. For a better
‍
quality check we can also provide the medical and export certificates if requested by customs when delivering the product to our THJ adopters in various places. Our product is top quality, rare, pure and organic raw honey, directly from the hive.
